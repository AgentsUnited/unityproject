POSE
rp -> relaxing pose
pp -> participating pose
sp -> straight pose

GESTURE
pre -> presenting
poi -> pointing
descr -> describing
think -> thinking
jh -> joint hands
twit -> twitching
takew -> taking the word
reac -> reacting

DIRECTION
self -> the actor toward himself
int -> the actor toward the interlocutor/s

SIDE
l -> left
r -> right

There are two recordings for each gesture, usually the second recording is more exagerated in the movement.
